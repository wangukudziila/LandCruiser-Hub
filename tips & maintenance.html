Tips & Maintenance

🔧 General Maintenance Tips

  • Change engine oil every 5,000–7,000 km
  • Top up coolant, brake, and transmission fluids monthly
  • Inspect tires and rotate every 10,000 km
  • Lubricate suspension and driveline components

🛠️ Model-Specific Maintenance

Land Cruiser 80 Series

  • Check axle seals for leaks
  • Grease front steering knuckles regularly
  • Diesel models: service injectors every 100,000 km

Land Cruiser 100 Series

  • Inspect AHC suspension system (if equipped)
  • Replace timing belt every 150,000 km
  • Extend rear axle breathers for water crossings

🚙 Off-Roading & Adventure Prep

  • Lower tire pressure to 18–22 PSI for sand or mud
  • Pack recovery tools: tow strap, shovel, compressor
  • Inspect undercarriage and skid plates before & after trails
  • Secure items inside vehicle or roof rack
