Popular Land Cruiser Models

Explore iconic Land Cruiser models beloved by off-road enthusiasts worldwide. Explore the evolution of the Lnacd Cruiser from the early 1990s to today, including the pPrado and Lexux LX series

Full-Size Land Cruiser Series

Land Cruiser 80 Series (1990–1997)

A rugged off-roader known for solid axles, full-time 4WD, and reliability. A favorite for overlanding.

Land Cruiser 100 Series (1998–2007)

Introduced luxury features and independent front suspension. Great for mixed use: city and trail.

Land Cruiser 200 Series (2008–2021)

More powerful with a V8 engine, improved safety features, and refined ride quality for long-distance travel.

Land Cruiser 300 Series (2021–Present)

The latest generation featuring a twin-turbo V6, modern infotainment, and next-gen off-road technology.

Land Cruiser Prado Series

Prado 90 Series (1996–2002)

Compact and capable, ideal for families who enjoy weekend adventures and light off-roading.

Prado 120 Series (2002–2009)

More refinement, better safety features, and popular in both urban and rural settings.

Prado 150 Series (2009–2023)

Modern design, fuel-efficient diesel options, and strong aftermarket support.

Prado 250 Series (2023–Present)

Newest release with retro styling, advanced suspension, and off-road drive modes. Shared platform with the 300 Series.

Lexus LX Series

Lexus LX 450 (1996–1997)

Luxury version of the 80 Series. Combines rugged performance with premium features.

Lexus LX 470 (1998–2007)

Based on the 100 Series. Offers smoother suspension and upgraded interiors.

Lexus LX 570 (2008–2021)

Luxury SUV based on the 200 Series. High-end comfort with V8 performance.

Lexus LX 600 (2022–Present)

Flagship Lexus SUV built on the 300 Series platform. Twin-turbo V6, luxury tech, and off-road capabilities.
