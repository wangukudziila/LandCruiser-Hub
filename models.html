Popular Land Cruiser Models

Explore the evolution of the Land Cruiser models beloved by off-road enthusiasts worldwide from the early 1990s to today, including the Prado and Pick UP series

Land Cruiser Prado Series

Prado 120 Series (2002–2009)

More refinement, better safety features, and popular in both urban and rural settings.

Prado 150 Series (2009–2023)

Modern design, fuel-efficient diesel options, and strong aftermarket support.

Prado 250 Series (2023–Present)

Newest release with retro styling, advanced suspension, and off-road drive modes. Shared platform with the 300 Series.

Land Cruiser PICKUP

Land Cruiser 75 series(1980-1990)

Single Cab & Troop Carrier Heavy-duty workhorse with leaf springs and solid axles. Built for extreme terrain and still trusted today

Land Cruiser 78 series(1990-Present)

Though often seen as a Troop Carrier, the 78 Series also includes utility pickup configurations. It's especially popular in Africa for its durability in remote conditions

Land Cruiser 79 Series (1999-Present)

The 79 Series is the main pickup variant of the 70 Series, available in both single and double cab. It is still in production and widely used in mining, agriculture, and military applications around the world

Land Cruiser 79 GR Sport(2021-Present)

The GR Sport variant is a performance-focused upgrade of the 79 Series, designed for rugged off-road performance with sporty enhancements. It’s equipped with Toyota’s Gazoo Racing styling and features

Note: The Land Cruiser 70 series pickup models are still in production and widely used in off-road and commercial environments.
