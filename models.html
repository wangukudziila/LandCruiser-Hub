Popular Land Cruiser Models

Explore iconic Land Cruiser models beloved by off-road enthusiasts worldwide.

Land Cruiser 40 Series (1960–1984)

A classic off-road icon, known for its rugged build and timeless design. A favorite among collectors.

Land Cruiser 60 Series (1980–1990)

Blending utility and comfort, the 60 Series introduced more passenger-focused features without compromising durability.

Land Cruiser 80 Series (1990–1997)

Celebrated for its superior off-road ability, solid axles, and advanced features for its time.

Land Cruiser 100 Series (1998–2007)

Introduced independent front suspension for better on-road comfort while retaining strong 4WD capabilities.

Land Cruiser 200 Series (2008–2021)

A modern powerhouse with luxury interior, advanced safety features, and world-class reliability.

Land Cruiser 300 Series (2021–Present)

The latest generation, boasting a lightweight chassis, turbocharged engines, and cutting-edge technology.
